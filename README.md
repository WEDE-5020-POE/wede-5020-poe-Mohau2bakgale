## Project Overview

A RE TSHWARANENG, which stands for “LET US UNITE ” is an NGO that operates in Mamelodi, Gauteng province. A RE TSHWARANENG was founded by Lehlohonolo Mohase, whose main objective when forming the NGO was to promote social cohesion, empowerment, and sustainable development of marginalized groups within the community. Ever since its establishment, the NGO has been active in various grassroots projects that include education, health, youth development, and poverty eradication initiatives.
The NGO was created under Lehlohonolo Mohase’s vision, which entails creating a platform that would foster social transformation through collective empowerment and action. It is clear from the organization’s activities and objectives that they believe community-driven empowerment is the best way to overcome the obstacles that members of Mamelodi face. A RE TSHWARANENG’s main target demographic includes disadvantaged youths, elderly, women, and other vulnerable groups in society.


## Website Goals and Objectives

The main objective of the website development project is the increased visibility and reachability of the NGO in order to secure additional funding for its work. The website will be used as the main source of communication and resources mobilization.
Specific goals include the following:

Increasing awareness of the ongoing, completed, and planned initiatives of the organisation.
Allowing people to make online donations in order to support community programmes.
Involving people into the community activities by organizing various events and volunteer opportunities.
Educating people with regard to various social and health topics relevant to the community.


## Timeline and Milestones

Week 1: Talking to the community, getting content for the website, and planning the layout.  
Week 2: Making wireframes, picking visual themes, and taking layout suggestions.  
Week 3: Making HTML pages, adding content, and adding features to the site
Week 4: Testing website on various devices and browsers; collecting feedback and launching the site.
